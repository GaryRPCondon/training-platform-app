# User Guide

## Dashboard
The dashboard provides an overview of your training status:
- **Total Distance**: All-time distance covered.
- **Active Plan**: Currently active training plan.
- **Phase Progress**: Current training phase, weekly volume, and upcoming milestones.
- **Weekly Breakdown**: Chart showing planned vs. actual distance for the current week.

## Training Calendar
- View your training schedule by month, week, or day.
- **Drag and Drop**: Click and drag a workout to a new date to reschedule it.
- **Workout Details**: Click on a workout to view details, including structured steps and targets.

## AI Coach
- Access the AI Coach via the "Chat" tab or sidebar.
- **New Chat**: Start a fresh conversation context.
- **History**: View and resume previous conversations from the sidebar.
- **Capabilities**: Ask about your plan, request modifications, or get advice on training methodology.

## Observations & Adjustments
- The system automatically monitors your training for issues (missed workouts, volume gaps, fatigue).
- **Observations**: Alerts appear on the dashboard. You can dismiss them or take action.
- **Adjustments**: The AI may propose adjustments (e.g., reducing volume, adding recovery).
  - **Accept**: Automatically modifies your future workouts.
  - **Reject**: Ignores the suggestion.

## Creating a Plan
1. Go to "Plans" -> "New Plan".
2. Enter your goal (e.g., "Marathon under 4 hours").
3. Select start date and preferences.
4. The AI will generate a comprehensive plan with phases and weekly schedules.
5. Review and "Activate" the plan to start training.
